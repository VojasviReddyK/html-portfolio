The Best Movies according to Vojasvi

My top 3 picks of all-time

HI NANNA

Hi Nanna is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film directed by Shouryuv, in his directoral debut, and produced by Vyra Entertainments.

HAPPY DAYS

Happy Days is a 2007 Indian Telugu -language musical coming of age film written, produced and directed by Sekhar Kammula.

ARJUN REDDY

Arjun Reddy is a 2017 Telugu romantic drama film starring Vijay Deverakonda and Shalini Pandey.
